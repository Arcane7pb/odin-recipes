Pà amb tomàquet

Description

Pà amb tomàquet is an easy and simple Catalan recipe. However there isn't a better way of accompanying some embutidos, which is basically some cured cold meat. Like Spanish jamón (ham), chorizo, italian mortadella among other fascinating embutidos.

The left side image is known as pà de coca or pa de vidre which is a long thin special baguette, when it's toasted is like crystal and the texture is amazing, it is the trend right now to use this bread when doing this recipe. The right side image is known as pà de pagès this one is the original bread used for this dish and it comes as a round fluffy bread before toasting,it has its' own charm.

Ingredients

  • Glass bread or country bread
  • Hanging tomatoes
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • (Optional) Salt
  • (Optional) Garlic

Steps

  1. Cut the bread. If your choice was glass bread, cut it horizontally, if it was country bread cut it vertically, make the cuts approximately 1cm from each other.
  2. (Optional) Peel the garlic and cut it in half, rub it all over the bread. This is more commonly done when using country bread.
  3. Cut the tomato in half and rub it all over the bread.
  4. Pour some oil on to the bread. To your liking, but try not to drench it too much.
  5. (Optional) Add a pinch of salt to the bread.

Now you are all set to enjoy your pà amb tomàquet, you can do so by eating the bread as it is, or accompanying it with some embutidos, cheese or some other Spanish tapas.
